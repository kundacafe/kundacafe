Established 2011 — Chiang Rai, Thailand

Founded in 2011, KUNDA CAFE is one of Thailand’s longest-operating independent plant-based restaurants. Located in the historic center of Chiang Rai, opposite Wat Ming Muang, the restaurant has evolved into a respected culinary destination for international travelers seeking refined vegan cuisine.

Global Recognition

Ranked among the world’s leading vegan restaurants and consistently recognized as one of Northern Thailand’s highest-rated dining establishments, KUNDA CAFE has welcomed guests from over 60 countries. Its sustained excellence over more than a decade reflects disciplined standards, ingredient integrity, and culinary precision.

Distinctions

  • Ranked Top 10 Vegan Restaurant Worldwide — TripAdvisor
  • Highest Rated Restaurant Koh Lanta Krabi Province (2013 ~ 2018)
  • Highest Rated Restaurant Chiang Rai Province (2018 ~ now)
  • South East Asian Plant-Based Burger Award Recipient 2022
  • Soy-Free Innovation Recognition 2023
  • Serving International Guests Since 2011

Independent. Refined. International.

KUNDA CAFE represents a modern interpretation of plant-based gastronomy — balancing creative independence with international credibility. With more than a decade of continuous operation, the restaurant stands as a benchmark for sustainable culinary entrepreneurship in Southeast Asia.
